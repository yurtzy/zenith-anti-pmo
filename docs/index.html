One-Click Setup

Follow these quick steps to safeguard your environment.

1

Extract the Suite

Download the ZIP archive and extract it to a directory on your machine (e.g. C:\Zenith or C:\Users\Username\Zenith).

2

Run Zenith Setup

Double-click zenith-setup.exe. Choose your path, configure components (Enable Shield and Watchdog), and click Install.

3

Configure Browser

Chrome will open automatically. Enable Developer Mode, click 'Load unpacked', paste the path, and check 'Allow in incognito'.
