Enabling Incognito Interception

By default, Google Chrome disables extensions in Incognito/Private tabs. To allow Zenith to keep you safe in private windows, follow these simple visual steps:

1

Open Extensions

Open a new tab and navigate to: chrome://extensions

2

Open Details

Find **Zenith Anti-PMO** in the list and click the **Details** button.

3

Toggle Switch

Scroll down to find **"Allow in Incognito"** and toggle the switch to **Enabled**.

Active System Bypass Protection

To prevent bypassing Zenith by launching other browsers (like Microsoft Edge or Firefox) or opening unprotected private windows, the windowless Zenith Shield application runs silently in the background. It monitors window titles for explicit triggers and terminates unauthorized activities instantly, redirecting you back to your Chrome breathing intervention page.

1

Launch the Shield

Navigate to the project folder and double-click zenith-shield.exe. It runs completely hidden (no console window will appear).

2

Setup Auto-Start

Press Win + R, type shell:startup, and press Enter. Right-click -> New Shortcut -> Browse and select the zenith-shield.exe file.

3

How to Disable

Open Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc), look for **zenith-shield** in the Background Processes list, and click **End Task**.
